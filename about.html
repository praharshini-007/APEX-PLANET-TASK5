Hello, I’m praharshini — a passionate web developer and AI enthusiast from Chennai, India.

My journey into tech began with curiosity and evolved into a deep commitment to creating impactful digital solutions.

I specialize in building clean, responsive, and accessible websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

With a strong foundation in AI and Data Science, I enjoy merging intelligent systems with beautiful interfaces.

I'm currently pursuing a B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science and actively working on multiple AI internships.

Over the last year, I’ve completed several hands-on projects, ranging from simple portfolio sites to AI-powered chatbots.

I believe that good design is invisible, and great interfaces should make users feel at home instantly.

I’ve worked extensively with Python, Numpy, Pandas, and Machine Learning libraries to build scalable data pipelines.

On the web development side, I love creating dynamic interfaces with modern JavaScript and CSS frameworks.

Whether it's building dashboards or deploying NLP apps, I focus on both performance and usability.

One of my core goals is to make AI more accessible through elegant and intuitive web apps.

Outside of code, I’m deeply fascinated by user psychology and how micro-interactions influence digital behavior.

I’m also a firm believer in open-source and love sharing my work on GitHub to help others learn.

My GitHub journey has helped me stay accountable, collaborate globally, and build a strong professional presence.

For me, learning never stops. Every project brings something new and exciting to master.
